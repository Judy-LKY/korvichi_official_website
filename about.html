About Korvichi

Innovative Products for Better Living

Founded in 2021, Korvichi designs, develops, and manufactures home organization products and consumer electronics for global customers.

Company Profile

Original design with dependable manufacturing

Korvichi is an innovative company specializing in home organization products and consumer electronics. We create practical, stylish, and high-quality products that enhance everyday life.

From acrylic coffee capsule organizers to LED educational drawing boards, every product is developed with creativity, precision engineering, and premium craftsmanship.

With strong OEM & ODM capabilities, advanced manufacturing facilities, and a professional R&D team, Korvichi has become a trusted partner for customers across Europe and North America.
